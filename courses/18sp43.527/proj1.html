Project #1: Racing Car Simulation

requirements

  • Divide the screen into two viewports. On the left, a "fixed" camera view should be rendered, which shows the overall scene. On the right, a "moving" camera view attached to the car should be rendered. (Refer to the screenshot above.)
  • The car can be controlled by arrow keys: left/right for rotation and up/down for forward/backward.
  • Color four walls with different colors to distinguish them.
  • Render the car whatever you want.
  • The size of the floor is 10x10.
  • The car cannot move outside the floor.
