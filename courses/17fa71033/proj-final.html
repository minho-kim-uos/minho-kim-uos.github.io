Final Project: “Searching Drone”

  • Objective: To implement interactive searching drone.
  • (Due: 21 Dec 11:59pm)
  • Requirements:
    • Implement a “drone” that can be controlled interactively.
      • The shape of the drone doesn't matter.
      • A UI (keyboard or mouse) to control the drone needs to be implemented. The user should be able to control the drone freely in the sky. The UI spec is as follows.:
        • UP: move forward
        • DOWN: move backward
        • LEFT/RIGHT: rotate around the vertical axis (yaw)
        • w/W: move up
        • s/S: move down
    • Implement (one or more) “search lights” attached to the drone.
      • By default, the search light(s) should be directed to the ground.
      • The lights should be modeled as spotlights.
      • The direction of the light(s) can be controlled by the user. But the multiple lights do not have to be controlled separately.
    • Implement a camera attached to the drone.
      • By default, the camera should be directed to the ground.
      • Render the scene viewed from the camera at the corner of the screen.
      • To verify the small screen, render the viewing frustum of the camera using lines only.
    • Make your program as fancy as possible!
