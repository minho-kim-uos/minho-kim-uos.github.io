course objectives
  • To understand the process of mathematical modelling in various fields.
  • To understand the numerical errors and the need of various numerical methods.
  • To understand and apply various numerical methods to find solutions of mathematical models.
  • To learn how to use numerical computing S/W, such as Matlab, GNU Octave, NumPy, etc.
