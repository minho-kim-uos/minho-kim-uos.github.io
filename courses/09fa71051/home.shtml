  • 9/10 Proving ∫(1/x)dx=log|x|.
    • For x>0,
      ∫(1/x)dx=logx.
    • For x<0, let y=-x then
      ∫(1/x)dx=∫(1/y)dy =logy=log(-x).
    Therefore, ∫(1/x)dx=log|x|.
