Term Project

  • Topic: Visualizing an algorithm (Bucket sort)
  • Team project (3~4 members)
  • Animation or game
  • 2D or 3D
  • Blender or OpenGL or both
  • Grading criteria
    • How effectively does it visualize the algorithm?
    • How effectively are the computer graphics used?
    • How advanced are the techniques? (camera work, lighting, modeling, transformations, etc.)
  • Grading: By the instructor and other teams
  • Peer review in each team
